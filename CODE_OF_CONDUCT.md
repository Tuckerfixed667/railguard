# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Be respectful.** Disagreements happen — focus on the technical merits. No personal attacks, trolling, or harassment.

**Be constructive.** When giving feedback, explain why something should change and suggest alternatives.

**Be welcoming.** Help newcomers get started. Answer questions patiently.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to ari@railyard.tech. All reports will be reviewed and responded to.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that violate this code of conduct.
